Settings

Configure your journal. Changes apply across the app.

Trading costs
Commissions
Per contract, per side. Minis (ES, NQ, RTY) and micros (MES, MNQ, M2K) are billed at different rates — the journal applies the right one automatically based on the instrument you traded.
Discipline guardrails
Your own rules. The Psychology report uses these to read your behavior — trading rapidly after you've broken your loss limit is what gets flagged as tilt, not just any loss after a loss. Rapid winners that protect capital aren't punished. Set these honestly.
Prop firms
Add the firms you trade with (Topstep, Lucid, etc.). Assign accounts to a firm, and the Report page breaks down spend by firm.
Brokers & margins
Add the brokers you clear through and define margin + round-trip fee per instrument. Personal accounts (and funded prop accounts) use these to size trades and to know when an account hits margin. Fee timing matters: some brokers (NinjaTrader) deduct fees the instant a trade closes; others (AMP) sweep the day's fees after the close — which can margin-call you on commissions you never saw intraday.
Journaling
Require emotional journaling
Pick the accounts where logging a trade requires the emotional-state step. Use it for accounts you're growing — your main or funded accounts — and leave it off for copy-followers so you're not journaling the same decision many times.
Integrations & appearance
OneNote notebook link
In OneNote: right-click the notebook or section → Copy Link. The "Open my OneNote" button on the Notes page uses this.
Account marker emoji
Marks your position on the path-to-passing bar on each account card.
